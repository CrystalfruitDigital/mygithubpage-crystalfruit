04 - The Logo

The #Boost logo meshes simplicity with material design and simple yet effective symbolism. It is a combination of the hash symbol ana round, yet sharp interpretation of the letter "B" and is meant to exude a bold, clean and modern look.


The charcoal gray and velvet red used portray luxury and wealth, which is what our service aims to bring to our clients. The simple two-color scheme, in tandem with the simple sharp design, also suggests that using the service will be straightforward and easy.

07 - ICONS


Inspired by Google's Material design, #Boost's icons aim to be bold, clean and modern. Ideally, icons will follow the #Boost color scheme and should be uniform in style.


Using simple, easily understand illustration and material depth, and designing icons that come with user interaction in mind when appropriate, will lead to greater user delight.

08 - PHOTOGRAPHY

#Boost Photography is essential to our design, and the photographs that are chosen to represent our brand should reflect all of our design choices.


From the "bold, clean, modern" mantra, to the color scheme, pictures that are used should look and feel like they are from the #Boost brand and thus should be instantly recognizable as such.


The composition should be easy to understand, with a strong focal point of (usually) velvet red, a clear focus of eye-catching subject matter and defined expressive content imbedded into the photograph's meaning
