About Us

PB&Jams, Philadelphia’s nut butter company, was founded by Megan Gibson – a high school health teacher and life-long athlete with a passion for great food, great music (the “jams” in PB&Jams!), and her community.

Our Mission

At PB&Jams, our motto is: “feed people, help people, and have fun!” Megan founded PB&Jams to blend four core passions:

;

Food

Nut butter and nut butter-inspired culinary pursuits.

Music

I DJ, and anywhere #TheCartEdition rolls, it brings jams, with a heavy biased towards funk, soul, feel good 80’s and golden-age hip-hop.

Education

Specifically nutrition and health, at the intersection of those in my community who need to learn about it most

Philadelphia

Philadelphia, where I grew up and where I make my home and where I seek to make a difference
