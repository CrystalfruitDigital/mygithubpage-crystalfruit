About Me

I’m Megan Gibson, founder of PB&Jams – Philadelphia’s nut butter company. I started PB&Jams because I wanted to blend three core passions:

Food

Nut butter and nut butter-inspired culinary pursuits.

Music

I DJ, and anywhere #TheCartEdition rolls, it brings jams, with a heavy biased towards funk, soul, feel good 80’s and golden-age hip-hop.

Education

Specifically nutrition and health, at the intersection of those in my community who need to learn about it most

Philadelphia

Philadelphia, where I grew up and where I make my home and where I seek to make a difference

I also started PB&Jams because I’m an entrepreneur from a family of entrepreneurs, and I’ve seen first hand how a small business can prove an unparalleled outlet for creative expression and making a difference in the community. So, as I was exploring ways to blend my passions of food, music and community service, PB&Jams became the logical next step. So I’m working to hand-craft precision blended nut butters while remaining a locally involved, locally loved and locally renowned company.
