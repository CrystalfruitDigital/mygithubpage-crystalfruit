The Problem

BCWD, or Backterial Cold-Water Disease, is a potentially lethal bacterial infection of salmonid fish species that spreads rapidly.

LEARN MORE

What does this Mean?

If not taken care of major ecosystems could take a devastating hit, and due to the highly contagious nature of the disease fish farms that provide food could have their supplies wiped out.

LEARN MORE

Our Solution
